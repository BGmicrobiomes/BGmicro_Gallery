Welcome to the BiomeFUN 2025 Workshop Gallery

The BiomeFUN2025 Workshop, held in Belgrade from 15-19 September 2025, brought together young researchers and experts in microbiome science and bioinformatics to share knowledge, gain practical skills, and build new collaborations.

This gallery captures the spirit of the event - insightful lectures, hands-on sessions, engaging discussions, and the friendships formed along the way.

A heartfelt thank you to all participants and lecturers for making this week of science and community truly memorable.
